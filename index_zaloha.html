About me
My name is Juraj Kebis, and I am a student of Computing Science at Utrecht University. I've started the studies in September 2022.
I was born in Bratislava, capital of Slovakia, and lived most of my childhood in a little town called Pezinok. At the age of 17 I had little knowledge about programming or how to even create an app, but my interest served as a catalyst for my motivation in becoming more skilled and gathering an education behind me, and since then, I have created multiple apps. One of these apps I have created is currently on the App store.
Education
At the age of 19, I graduated from Zochova 9, a secondary school in Bratislava. In September 2018, I started my Bachelor's study at University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Trnava, where I studied Applied Informatics for 3 years. During the study, I was also a student senator for the Faculty Council. I graduated my Bachelor's degree in June 2021, and after that, I continued my Master's degree in the same field.

With the first year of master's, which started in September 2021, I started an Erasmus exchange in Utrecht, where I was for period of one year. I've decided to make a change and I started studying full master's course Computing Science at Utrecht University in September 2022.

Projects & Experience
My first full programming experience started in 2017, when I was working as a PLC junior Programmer in Volkswagen, Bratislava. There, I've learned a lot about programming and how fascinating that field is.
In years after, during my Bachelor degree, I was part of many team groups, where we worked on various projects. One of the projects I worked on was History-Pexeso, which is a cross-platform educational game.

Next to my Bachelor study, I started developing iOS and MacOS apps. Currently, there are 4 apps that I have worked on, from which 2 are finished, and one is published on the App Store. You can read more about them under the Projects tab.
